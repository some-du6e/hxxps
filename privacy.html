Privacy Policy

hxxps

Last updated: March 7, 2026

The short version

hxxps does not collect, store, transmit, or share any data about you or your browsing activity. Ever.

What hxxps does

hxxps is a browser extension that converts defanged / obfuscated URLs (e.g. hxxps://example[.]com ) into normal, openable links via your context menu. All processing happens entirely within your browser.

Data collection

hxxps collects no data whatsoever . Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected.
  • No browsing history or URLs are stored or transmitted.
  • No analytics or telemetry of any kind.
  • No third-party services are contacted.

Permissions explained

hxxps requests only the minimum permissions needed:

  • contextMenus — to add options to your right-click menu.
  • scripting / activeTab — to paste converted text into the active field when you use the context menu.
  • clipboardWrite — to copy a converted link to your clipboard.
  • tabs / windows — to open a deobfuscated link in a new tab or incognito window.

None of these permissions are used to read, record, or transmit any data.
